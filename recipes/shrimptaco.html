Shrimp Taco

Recipe

What you'll need

  • Extra Jumbo Shrimp
  • Onion
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Zero Carb Taco Tortilla
  • Salt, Pepper, Paprika
  • Choice of Hot Sauce
  • Cauliflower Rice

Steps

  1. Slice and grill onions
  2. Peel and season the shrimp and throw it in with the onion
  3. After the shrimp pinkens, throw in your bean sprouts
  4. Throw in green onions and sesame oil for extra taste
  5. Eat it with cauliflower rice and zero carb tortilla for a calorie conscious way to get full
