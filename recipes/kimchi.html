Kimchi Fried Rice

Recipe

What you'll need

  • Day old rice
  • Kimchi
  • Pork Belly
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Gochujang(고추장)

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan with some cooking oil.
  2. Fry your pork belly of meat of choice. No need for extra oil!
  3. After your meat is seared, throw in your kimchi (8oz), gochujang (1tbsp), and soy sauce(1tbsp)
  4. Grill until the mixture thickens.
  5. Throw in your rice! I like to throw in sesame oil to add layers to the flavor and an egg on top. Enjoy!
